### La Liga Player Analysis
![La Liga Clusters](assets/laliga_clusters.png) <!--  -->

**One-Liner**: Analysis of La Liga football players using Machine Learning techniques to identify player types and similarities.

**Tech Stack**: **Python**, **Pandas**, **Scikit-learn**, **Matplotlib**, **Selenium**

**Key Features**:
- **Dimensionality Reduction**: Using PCA (Principal Component Analysis) to simplify 27 attributes into key components.
- **Unsupervised Learning**: K-Means clustering to group players based on performance stats rather than positions.
- **Data Scraping**: Custom Selenium scrapers to collect player statistics from the web.

**The "Why"**: To discover objective player similarities and classifications purely from data, removing human bias and traditional position labels.
